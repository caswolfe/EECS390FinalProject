Village Evacuation

Village Evacuation is a top-down, 2D dungeon crawler based on a fictionalized Case Western Reserve University campus during the COVID-19 outbreak. The objective of the game is to free students from the clutches of COVID-19 viruses (depicted as cartoon bacteriophages), make their way through the 7 village houses, and defeat a boss. Group members: Alexander Sfakianos (aks164), Chad Wolfe (csw52), Andrew Szabo (abs93), Cam Farmer (ccf35)

Controls
W to move UP
A to move LEFT
S to move DOWN
D to move RIGHT
MOUSE1 to Interact with NPCs, Doors, and other interactable objects
